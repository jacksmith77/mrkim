Kim's Convenience


This show is by far the funniest show I have seen in a long time. It will have you hooked with characters and storyline from the jump. Although it may not be for everyone I do believe that if given a chance you will be a fan too. It has views of all walks of life and touches base on issues that go on from day to day while making you laugh. We have so much to learn from Mr. Kim.
